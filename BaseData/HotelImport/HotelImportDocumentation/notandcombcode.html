Documentation: 
The combination code of the price lines with which the actual price line may not be combined.
If this price line is a SO, and cannot be combined with an EB, then this CombCode=SO, NotAndCombCode=E . There will be other price lines where CombCode=EB. If there are other EB price lines with the CombCode=EB in the pricing, and this SO price line, then this price line will not be calculated in the pricing, as this price line cannot be used when there is an EB in the pricing.
