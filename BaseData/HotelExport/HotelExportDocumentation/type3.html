Documentation: 
Describes which amounts for the day prices need to be used when determining the special offer amount, e.g.
Day1 - 100 CHF
Day2 - 110 CHF
Day3 - 120CHF
Day4 - 130 CHF
Day5  - 140 CHF
Day6  - 150 CHF
Day7  - 160 CHF
If the Type is EndDays, and I have a valid 7/5, then the special offer will be 150 + 160 CHF.
